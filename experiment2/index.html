Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Reference Image

Image made by wombmates

Description

Created a generator that creates a living impression based on a piece of landscape art.

Technical

Utilizes for loops and noise to randomly generated terrain within draw(), filling their shape with a color variable. Flowers are created with a nested for loop, creating multiple instances of flower patterns. These flowers, and additionaly the sun and clouds, move on their X axis and are influenced by mouse input.

Reflection

I once again struggled with this one a bit, but I do have a sense I’m getting better at mimicking and almost working backwards to understand code. I feel as long as I have code examples in future projects I’ll be ok. I think my living impression turned out pretty well, and I’m proud of the flowers and clouds I created.
